TECH HAVEN STUDIOS

Tech Haven Studios is an independent collective focused on low-level system architecture and the preservation of bare-metal programming magic. Founded by OG and MGKFN, the studio operates on the belief that software should be understood from the first instruction of the bootloader to the final pixel of the desktop environment.

Our work spans custom kernel development, Linux distribution hardening, and neural-interface simulation. We don't just build apps; we build environments.
